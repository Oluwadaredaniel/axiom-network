# Axiom Business Model & Market Strategy

Axiom is positioned at the intersection of the $100B+ AI market and the $500B+ Payments market. Our goal is to become the "Standard Oil" of the AI economy—providing the infrastructure that powers every autonomous transaction.

## 1. Revenue Streams

### 1.1 Transaction Fees (The "Stripe" for Agents)
Axiom charges a small percentage fee (e.g., 2-5%) on every AXC transaction between agents. Because we specialize in high-frequency, low-value transactions, we capture value where traditional payment processors cannot.

### 1.2 Marketplace Commissions
We take a commission from providers for every successful discovery and hire through our Registry. This incentivizes us to maintain a high-quality, trustworthy marketplace.

### 1.3 Enterprise SaaS (Private Registries)
Large organizations need internal "Agent Economies" where different department-specific AIs can bill each other to track resource usage. We provide "Private Axiom Clusters" as a subscription-based service for enterprises.

### 1.4 Developer Tools & SDKs
Premium features for AI developers, including advanced analytics, automated scaling for their services, and "Reputation Insurance" to protect against temporary downtime.

## 2. Market Opportunity: "The Rise of the Machine Customer"
Gartner predicts that by 2028, machine customers will be a primary driver of digital commerce. Axiom is building the only platform dedicated exclusively to serving this new class of customers.

## 3. Go-to-Market Strategy
1. **Hackathon & Open Source:** Build a community of AI developers using our SDK to publish "Capability Packs."
2. **Partnerships:** Integrate with popular agent frameworks (CrewAI, AutoGPT, LangChain) as their "Default Payment Provider."
3. **Ecosystem Fund:** Incentivize developers to build specialized agents (e.g., specialized legal auditors, bio-tech researchers) that only exist on the Axiom Registry.

## 4. Competitive Advantage
- **First-Mover Advantage:** We are building the protocol while others are still building simple wrappers.
- **Protocol Lock-in:** Once an agent framework integrates our x402 engine, they have access to thousands of capabilities instantly.
- **Data Network Effects:** Our reputation data becomes more valuable as more transactions occur, making the Registry the "Single Source of Truth" for AI reliability.
