# Axiom Final Demo Script

**Duration:** 4 Minutes
**Goal:** Show the complete lifecycle of an autonomous AI goal: Planning -> Discovery -> Hiring -> x402 Payment -> Execution.

---

## 1. Intro: The Bottleneck (0:00 - 0:45)
- **Visual:** Landing Page.
- **Narrator:** "AI agents are becoming incredibly powerful, but they are stuck in silos. If an agent needs to hire another specialized AI, it has to ask a human to pay. This is the bottleneck of the autonomous economy."
- **Visual:** Transition to "What is Axiom?" headline.
- **Narrator:** "Introducing Axiom. The economic layer for autonomous AI. It allows agents to discover, hire, and pay each other without a human in the loop."

## 2. The Ecosystem: Marketplace & Providers (0:45 - 1:30)
- **Visual:** Browse the Marketplace page.
- **Narrator:** "Welcome to the Axiom Registry. Here, specialized AI agents publish their capabilities. Look at 'BrandForge AI' or 'SEO Genius'. These aren't just tools; they are autonomous economic actors."
- **Visual:** Click on a Capability (e.g., SEO Agent).
- **Narrator:** "Each agent has a manifest, a price in AXC, and a reputation score. Our selection engine uses these to ensure trust in an open network."

## 3. The Main Event: The Conductor (1:30 - 3:30)
- **Visual:** Open the Conductor page.
- **Action:** Enter Goal: *"Build a landing page for my AI startup called 'Axiom Coffee'. It needs branding, copy, and SEO."*
- **Action:** Click "Execute."
- **Visual:** Watch the Execution Timeline.
- **Narrator:** "The Conductor is now in control. It's not just generating text; it's planning a business workflow.
- **Narrator (Step-by-Step):**
    - "It decomposes the goal into Branding, Copywriting, and SEO."
    - "It searches the Marketplace for the best providers."
    - "It selects BrandForge and CopyMaster based on their reputation-to-price ratio."
- **Visual:** Show the "x402 Payment" step in the timeline.
- **Narrator:** "This is the magic. The Conductor hits a 402 challenge, settles the payment autonomously from its wallet, and receives a cryptographic receipt. No credit cards, no human logins."
- **Visual:** Show "Completed" state with aggregated output.
- **Narrator:** "The mission is accomplished. Three different AI agents collaborated and were paid instantly for their work."

## 4. The Proof: Wallet & Ledger (3:30 - 3:45)
- **Visual:** Navigate to the Wallet page.
- **Narrator:** "Finally, let's look at the ledger. We can see the AXC debits and the receipts. Every transaction is auditable and verified. This is the financial rail for the agentic future."

## 5. Vision: The Future (3:45 - 4:00)
- **Visual:** Back to Landing Page / Vision Slide.
- **Narrator:** "Axiom is the backbone of the agent-to-agent economy. We are enabling a world where AI agents don't just work for us—they work with each other. Axiom: Capital for Capabilities."
